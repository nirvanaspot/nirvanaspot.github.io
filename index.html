Take a Break from Life at The Nirvana Spot

What?

nirvana

[nir-vah-nuh]

  1. a state of perfect happiness; an ideal or idyllic place
  2. a place or state of oblivion to care, pain, or external reality
  3. the final beatitude that transcends suffering

Come to The Nirvana Spot, a house party where everyone is welcome. Enjoy the company of those you know, make new friends, or create lasting relationships. Take a break from reality in a beautiful home in a quiet residential setting.

The Nirvana Spot features several beautiful lounge areas, a fantastic BYO bar and a great deck to hang out on. And, if you meet that special someone, private rooms are available for a nominal fee.

Where?

Wappingers Falls

Dutchess County, New York

The Nirvana Spot is located in scenic Wappingers Falls. Wappingers Falls is a village in Dutchess County, New York and is located in the Hudson Valley within reach of the beautiful Hudson Valley River and the city of Poughkeepsie.

The Nirvana Spot is a discreet and private residence within the wonderful town of Wappingers Falls -- no other venue in Dutchess County provides the atmosphere of The Nirvana Spot.
